## Tone of Voice

- **Warm and welcoming** — Guests are family; the writing should feel like Daout himself greeting you at the door
- **Quietly authentic** — Never boastful; French heritage is shown, not advertised
- **Sensory and evocative** — Describe food in a way that makes you taste it before you read the menu
- **Grounded in place** — Piermont, the village, the Hudson — the location is part of the identity

# Brand Style — Sidewalk Bistro

**Client:** Sidewalk Bistro  
**Location:** Piermont, NY (Hudson Valley / Hudson River waterfront)  
**Owner:** Daout Celestin  
**Positioning:** Authentic French bistro — "Paris in Piermont"  
**Generated from:** Social performance baseline review (June 2026). Update after client brand onboarding session.

---

## Brand Identity

**Tagline / Core idea:** Paris in Piermont — the warmth, technique, and intimacy of a classic French bistro, anchored in a Hudson Valley village.

**Voice:** Warm, confident, unpretentious. Evocative and sensory. Slightly poetic but never precious. The chef knows their craft; the brand wears it lightly.

**Tone notes:**
- French authenticity language performs well — lean into it ("Joyeux," "à table," "comme d'habitude")
- Sensory framing works: smell, texture, temperature, ritual
- Community-embedded: Piermont village, the Hudson River, the arts scene, local provenance
- Avoid: corporate language, discounting language, "fresh ingredients" clichés

---

## Signature Formats

- **Single image:** Hero food photography. Warm, moody bistro lighting. Overhead or slight angle.
- **Carousel:** Educational content (French food culture, aperitif guides), event recaps, community stories. Max 8 slides.
- **Reel (30–60 sec):** Kitchen process, ambience, prep moments. No narration needed — ambient kitchen audio or French music. Primary reach/discovery format.
- **Stories:** BYOW Wednesday engagement polls, guest UGC reposts, reservation reminders. Daily when feasible.

---

## Signature Content Series

**BYOW Wednesday:** Every Wednesday — feature the Bring Your Own Wine tradition. Encourage guests to tag @sidewalk_bistro. Run a Stories poll ("What are you bringing tonight?"). Goal: build UGC pool and drive Wednesday bookings.

---

## Key Business Differentiators (to thread into content)

1. **Authentic French technique** — crêpe suzette, steak frites, classic bistro execution
2. **BYOW Wednesdays** — rare in the Hudson Valley; a genuine community ritual
3. **Piermont waterfront village** — location as part of the brand narrative
4. **"Paris in Piermont" positioning** — no competitor owns this

## Audience

- **Core:** 30–60+ local Hudson Valley diners, Piermont village regulars, NYC weekenders
- **Growth target:** Younger foodies (25–40) via Reels and TikTok; Gen Z via French food content

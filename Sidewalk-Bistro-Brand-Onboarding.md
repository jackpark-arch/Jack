## Part 1 — A Few Things Only You Can Tell Us

We keep this short. These five answers shape everything we create.

**1. Who is your ideal guest?**
Describe the person you most want walking through the door — who they are, where they're coming from, what they care about. (One or two sentences is perfect.)

*For example: "Couples and friends from Rockland County and NYC who want an escape that feels special without being stuffy — people who appreciate real French cooking and a genuine welcome."*

---

**2. Which dishes should we feature most on social?**
Your "hero" items — the things you'd put front and centre if you had a billboard. Which 3–5 dishes best represent Sidewalk Bistro?

---

**3. What makes you different?**
There are French restaurants in the Hudson Valley. Why do your regulars choose you over them, and what would you want new guests to understand about you that they wouldn't get from a menu alone?

---

**4. What do you want social media to do for your business?**
Pick 1–2 that apply:

- [ ] Bring in new guests who haven't heard of us yet (awareness)
- [ ] Drive reservations and covers (direct sales / bookings)
- [ ] Build a loyal community of regulars online
- [ ] Promote specific nights, events, or programmes (BYOW, brunch, specials)
- [ ] Showcase the team and kitchen (behind-the-scenes / culture)
- [ ] Attract press, features, or collaborations

---

**5. Where are you now with social?**
- How often are you posting currently?
- What's been working — any posts or formats that got great responses?
- What's felt flat or time-consuming?
- Is there anything you wish you were doing but haven't gotten to yet?

## Part 2 — Assets to Send Us

These are the ingredients we need to produce content that actually looks like *you* — not a generic restaurant template.

### Must-Haves

| Asset | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Brand colours** | Hex codes if you have them; descriptions are fine too (e.g. "deep burgundy, cream, forest green") |
| **Font names** | What font(s) appear on your menus, signage, or website? |

| **Food photos — hero dishes** | Original high-resolution files (not screenshots). One clean shot per dish you want us to feature. *This is the single most important visual asset — AI-approximations of food don't look like your food.* |

### Helpful to Have

| Asset | Notes |
